Major Government Schemes for Farmers

e-NAM (National Agricultural Market)

A unified online market for agricultural commodities.

  • Connects 1000+ APMC mandis nationwide
  • Offers better price discovery for farmers
  • Reduces middlemen and promotes direct selling
  • Real-time trading and mobile app support

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund

Provides medium-long term loans for post-harvest infrastructure.

  • Interest subvention of 3% per annum
  • Credit guarantee coverage for eligible borrowers
  • Supports cold storage, silos, warehouses, etc.
  • Tenure: Up to 7 years for loan repayment

PM Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

A comprehensive crop insurance scheme for farmers.

  • 2% premium for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi crops
  • 100% claim coverage in case of crop loss
  • Satellite and drone-based crop monitoring
  • Mandatory for loanee farmers
Explore More

PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana

A voluntary pension scheme for small and marginal farmers.

  • ₹3,000 monthly pension after age 60
  • Contribution starts from ₹55/month (age 18)
  • Government matches the farmer’s contribution
  • Beneficiaries covered under LIC pension plan
Explore More

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Income support of ₹6,000/year for landholding farmers.

  • Paid in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000
  • Transferred directly to bank accounts
  • Simple Aadhaar-based registration
  • Open to all farmers (except institutional landowners)
Explore More

PM Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

Promotes efficient water use in agriculture.

  • Focus on micro-irrigation (drip/sprinkler)
  • Enhances farm-level water use efficiency
  • Supports watershed management
  • Subsidy available for installing irrigation systems
Explore More

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides detailed soil reports to farmers.

  • Issued every 2 years
  • Includes 12 key nutrient values
  • Suggests corrective fertilizer usage
  • Improves yield through better soil management
Explore More

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

A flexible scheme to fund state-level agri-development projects.

  • State-specific targets based on need
  • Focus on technology and research adoption
  • Supports organic farming, seeds, irrigation, etc.
  • Encourages public-private partnerships in agri-infra
Explore More

National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

Promotes sustainable practices in agriculture.

  • Focus on climate-resilient farming
  • Includes rainwater harvesting and agroforestry
  • Promotes Integrated Nutrient Management (INM)
  • Supports organic & traditional farming methods
