Transport Resources for Farmers

🔰 Government Initiatives & Subsidies

  • Subsidies on cold storage and transportation under the PM Kisan SAMPADA Yojana.
  • Assistance from NABARD for rural road and transport development.
  • Agri-logistics grants for FPOs (Farmer Producer Organizations).

🛵 Local & Affordable Delivery Options (Ideal for ₹500 - ₹2000 Deliveries)

Dunzo (Small Parcel Delivery)
Ideal for intra-city deliveries of farm produce, seeds, tools, or fertilizers.
Pickup by bike within 60 minutes.
Visit Dunzo
Uber Connect
Affordable courier and parcel delivery using Uber drivers.
Works via Uber app and available in most cities.
Visit Uber Connect
WeFast / Borzo
Ultra-fast courier service using scooters, great for seeds, tools, and small packages.
Supports same-day delivery across towns.
Visit Borzo

❄️ Cold Chain & Storage Support

  • Integrated Cold Chain Scheme – support for refrigerated vans and storage.
  • Agro-Logistics platforms offering real-time monitoring.
  • Subsidy on solar-powered cold rooms for remote areas.

🧰 Useful Tools & Helplines

  • Kisan Call Center: Dial 1800-180-1551 for transport-related help.
  • eNAM: Offers price discovery and trade logistics integration. Visit eNAM
  • GeM (Govt. e-Marketplace): For procurement of transport vehicles and services. Visit GeM
